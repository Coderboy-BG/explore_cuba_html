The Cuban Beaches

Cuba’s beaches are celebrated for their white sands, turquoise waters, and serene atmosphere. Top destinations include Varadero (22 km of resort-lined coast), Cayo Coco and Cayo Guillermo (ideal for snorkeling and diving), Guardalavaca (lush and tranquil), Playa Ancón (near historic Trinidad), and Cayo Santa María (secluded luxury). With over 5,700 km of coastline, Cuba offers uncrowded shores, rich marine biodiversity, and a tropical climate that makes it a year-round paradise for beach lovers.
